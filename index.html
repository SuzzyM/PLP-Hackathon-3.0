Stylistic Devices

Stylistic devices are writing techniques used to enhance a writers work. They are also referred to as: Literary Devices. Writers use stylistic devices to enrich their work and lift it beyond literal meaning.

Examples of stylistic devices:

  1. Metaphor
  2. Simile
  3. Imagery
  4. Symbolism
  5. Personification
  6. Hyperbole
